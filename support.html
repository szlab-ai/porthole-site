Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign in?

Porthole uses your existing Telegram account. On first launch, enter your phone number and the login code Telegram sends you. Porthole never sees or stores your password.

How do I report or block content?

Open a message and use its action menu to Report or Block, or leave a chat. Objectionable content can also be reported to us by email.

What do I need to run Porthole?

An Apple TV running tvOS 17 or later, and a Telegram account.

Is this the official Telegram app?

No. Porthole is an independent, unofficial client and is not affiliated with or endorsed by Telegram.

Report Objectionable Content

Use the in-app report/block controls, or email szlab.ai@outlook.com with details. We act on reports promptly.

常见问题

如何登录?

Porthole 使用你已有的 Telegram 账户。首次启动时输入手机号及 Telegram 发送的登录验证码。Porthole 不会看到或存储你的密码。

如何举报或屏蔽内容?

打开某条消息，通过其操作菜单选择举报或屏蔽，也可退出会话。不良内容也可邮件反馈给我们。

运行 Porthole 需要什么?

一台运行 tvOS 17 或更高版本的 Apple TV，以及一个 Telegram 账户。

这是官方 Telegram 应用吗?

不是。Porthole 是独立、非官方的客户端，与 Telegram 无隶属或背书关系。

举报不良内容

请使用应用内的举报/屏蔽功能，或邮件联系 szlab.ai@outlook.com 并附上详情。我们会及时处理。
