Porthole — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

Overview

Porthole is an unofficial, independent application for viewing Telegram on Apple TV. This policy explains how Porthole handles your information. In short: Porthole does not collect it.

Data We Collect

None. The developer operates no servers and has no access to your messages, contacts, media, or account. We do not collect, receive, store off your device, or sell any personal data.

Data Stored on Your Device

To function, Porthole stores your Telegram login session, a local cache of messages and media, and your app settings. This data is kept only on your Apple TV, managed by Telegram's official library (TDLib), and is not transmitted to the developer.

Connection to Telegram

Porthole connects directly to Telegram's servers. Your use of Telegram — including the messages and content you access — is governed by Telegram's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. Porthole is not affiliated with or endorsed by Telegram.

No Tracking or Analytics

Porthole contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking software. It does not track you across other apps or websites, and it uses no advertising identifier.

Children

Porthole is not directed to children and is rated for mature audiences due to the unrestricted nature of third-party content available on Telegram.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email szlab.ai@outlook.com.

Porthole — 隐私政策

最后更新：2026 年 7 月 4 日

概述

Porthole 是一款非官方、独立开发的、用于在 Apple TV 上查看 Telegram 的应用。本政策说明 Porthole 如何处理你的信息。简而言之：Porthole 不收集你的信息。

我们收集的数据

无。开发者不运营任何服务器，无法访问你的消息、联系人、媒体或账户。我们不收集、不接收、不在你的设备之外存储、也不出售任何个人数据。

存储在你设备上的数据

为实现功能，Porthole 会存储你的 Telegram 登录会话、消息与媒体的本地缓存以及应用设置。这些数据仅保存在你的 Apple TV 上，由 Telegram 官方库（TDLib）管理，不会传输给开发者。

与 Telegram 的连接

Porthole 直接连接 Telegram 服务器。你对 Telegram 的使用（包括你访问的消息与内容）受 Telegram 隐私政策 与 服务条款 约束。Porthole 与 Telegram 无隶属或背书关系。

无追踪与分析

Porthole 不含任何第三方分析、广告或追踪软件，不跨应用或网站追踪你，也不使用广告标识符。

儿童

Porthole 不面向儿童；鉴于 Telegram 上第三方内容不受限，本应用面向成年受众。

变更

若本政策变更，更新版本将连同新日期发布于本页。
